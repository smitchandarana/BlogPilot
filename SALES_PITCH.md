### What It Does (In Plain English)

**Every 20 minutes, BlogPilot:**

1. 📱 **Scans your LinkedIn feed** → finds relevant posts from your target topics
2. 🧠 **Scores each post with AI** → only engages with high-quality content (8-10 score threshold)
3. 💬 **Writes genuine comments** → unique, thoughtful, no templates
4. ❤️ **Engages strategically** → likes posts, posts comments, connects with relevant people
5. 📧 **Finds emails** → uses pattern matching, SMTP verification, Hunter.io to capture lead emails
6. 📊 **Tracks everything** → analytics dashboard shows what's working

**All while you focus on real work.** BlogPilot runs in the background, enforces daily budgets, avoids LinkedIn detection, and logs every action.

## How It Works (The Technical Part)

**Local-first architecture** = **Your data never leaves your machine**

```
Your Computer (Runs BlogPilot)
    ├─ Backend (Python + FastAPI)
    │   ├─ LinkedIn automation (Playwright browser)
    │   ├─ AI layer (Groq API for comments/posts)
    │   ├─ Email enrichment (DOM scraper + pattern matching)
    │   └─ SQLite database (your data, encrypted)
    │
    └─ Frontend (React dashboard)
        ├─ Real-time monitoring
        ├─ Configuration UI
        ├─ Analytics & exports
        └─ One-click start/stop
```

**Privacy guaranteed.** No tracking, no cloud servers, no data selling. Your LinkedIn credentials stay encrypted on your machine.

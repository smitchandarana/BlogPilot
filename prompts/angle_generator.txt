INPUT FIELDS:

Mistake: {mistake}
False belief: {false_belief}
Contradiction: {contradiction}
Scenario: {scenario}

- Arguable: a real practitioner could disagree with it
- Specific: names a role, action, or outcome — not "people should think differently"
- Engagement-ready: designed to get a reply from someone who lives this problem daily

Angle types to choose from:
- contrarian: Directly challenges a standard practice
- mistake: Names a specific costly error the audience makes
- hidden_cost: Reveals an overlooked consequence of a common approach
- trend_shift: Before/after frame — names exactly what changed and who hasn't caught up
- system_failure: Shows how a process or tool fails the people using it

Select best_angle_index = the angle most likely to generate a reply from a real practitioner.

Return ONLY valid JSON, no preamble, no markdown:
{{"angles": [{{"type": "contrarian|mistake|hidden_cost|trend_shift|system_failure", "stance": "<one arguable sentence naming a specific role or outcome>", "rationale": "<one sentence: why this angle creates engagement>"}}], "best_angle_index": 0}}

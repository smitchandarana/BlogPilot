Score each LinkedIn comment candidate. Be harsh — most AI-generated comments are mediocre.

Scoring criteria (each 0-2, total 0-10):

  specificity:      References a specific concrete detail (number, tool, scenario, named consequence), not just the general topic
  adds_something:   Says something NEW the author didn't already say (not a rephrase, not a summary)
  human_voice:      Sounds like a real person typing mid-conversation — casual, direct, no corporate polish
  reply_worthy:     Author would genuinely want to respond with something substantive (not just "thanks!")
  no_ai_smell:      Zero AI tells (see AI TELLS list below)

AI TELLS — any of these automatically sets no_ai_smell=0:
  - Starts with "In my experience..." / "From my experience..." / "In my work..."
  - Starts with "I've seen..." / "I've found..." / "I've noticed..."
  - Uses "resonates" / "this resonates" / "really resonates"
  - Uses "actionable" / "actionable insights" / "actionable steps"
  - Uses "game-changer" / "paradigm shift" / "moving the needle"
  - Uses "at the end of the day" / "it's worth noting" / "it's important to note"
  - Structured like a mini-essay with an intro sentence + explanation + conclusion
  - Ends with an open-ended question ("What are your thoughts?", "Would love to hear more", "How do you see this?")
  - Ends with a compliment followed by a question ("Great insight! Have you considered...")
  - References the author in third person ("The author mentions...", "James talks about...")

AUTOMATIC REJECT (set total=0, fill reject_reason):
  - Refers to the author in third person ("Author mentions…", "Name talks about…", "The post says...")
  - Contains "can you elaborate", "I'd love to hear", "can you share more", "what are your thoughts"
  - Just summarizes the post and adds a generic question at the end
  - Starts with any form of "Great post" / "Love this" / "Totally agree" / "So true"
  - Opens with "In my experience" / "From my experience" / "I've seen this"
  - Fabricates a statistic that doesn't exist (e.g., "studies show 73%...")
  - Contains hollow phrases: "game-changer", "paradigm shift", "resonates deeply", "so important"
  - Ends with an open-ended question ("What do you think?", "Would love to hear your thoughts")

WHAT SCORES HIGH:
  - Opens directly with a concrete observation, number, or named scenario
  - Adds one specific thing the post didn't say (not more — one sharp thing)
  - Has one narrow follow-up question IF the question is specific and answerable in one sentence
  - Sounds like it was typed in 30 seconds by someone who lived through the situation
  - Under 60 words and says more than most 200-word AI comments
